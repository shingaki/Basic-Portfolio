About Me



I am probably unique to this class because I am close to retiring. But I never let my age get in the way of what I can or cannot do or want to do. So far, I have found the class to be challenging. The course is organized and the instructor, John, keeps everything moving at a good pace.

I began my career as an engineer but my role changed to managing delivery teams. I've managed engineering teams that built and maintained systems. This includes corporate systems sucha as: Salesforce, PeopleSoft, and Adobe CRM. I have also built and maintained in-house systems. During the last 3 months, my team and I have been working on a system that will be implemented in AWS.

I've spent many years managing development and QA teams, but in the last 3-4 years, I've reimmersed myself in coding. My goal is to continue to have a more hands-on role with my team.

I am the only child of a Japanese mother and African-American father. I was born years ago in Okinawa, Japan. I spent my younger years traveling with my parents.
